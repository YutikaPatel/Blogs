Flower Crop Profit Model: Solan and Pune/Mumbai

By Yutika • Updated April 29, 2026

🏔️ SOLAN FLOWER LIST (COOL CLIMATE - HIGH PROFIT MODEL)

🌺 Carnation (CORE BUSINESS CROP) - 7

  • ⏱️ Growth: 5-6 months
  • 🌸 Harvest: every 7-10 days
  • 🔁 Life: 2-3 years continuous
  • 📦 Yield: 150-200 bundles/month/tunnel
  • 💰 Price: ₹200-350/bundle
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹4-7 lakh/year/tunnel
  • 🧠 Best planting: Sep-Oct
  • ⭐ Profit: Very high + stable

🌷 Oriental Lily (PREMIUM) - 5

  • ⏱️ Growth: 90-120 days
  • 🔁 Cycles: 2-3/year
  • 📦 Yield: ~10,000 stems/polyhouse
  • 💰 Price: ₹80-150/stem
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹8-15 lakh/cycle
  • 🧠 Planting: staggered year-round
  • ⚠️ High investment

🌸 Eustoma (Lisianthus) - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: 5-6 months
  • 🌸 Harvest: multiple flushes
  • 💰 Price: ₹15-40/stem
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹5-8 lakh/year
  • ⚠️ Sensitive crop

🌿 Gypsophila (FILLER - HIGH DEMAND) - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: ~4 months
  • 🌸 Harvest: continuous
  • 💰 Price: ₹200-400/bunch
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹4-8 lakh/year
  • ⭐ High demand in bouquets

🌿 Limonium (DRY FLOWER MARKET) - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: 4-5 months
  • 🌸 Harvest: continuous
  • 💰 Price: ₹150-300/bunch
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹3-6 lakh/year
  • ⭐ Long shelf life

🌼 Chrysanthemum - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: 3-4 months
  • 🌸 Harvest: seasonal
  • 💰 Price: ₹80-200/bundle
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹2-4 lakh/cycle
  • 🧠 Best for festival timing

🌺 Gladiolus - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: 3-4 months
  • 🔁 Cycles: 2-3/year
  • 💰 Price: ₹5-15/stem
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹1-2 lakh/cycle

🥬 Ornamental Kale - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: 2-3 months
  • ❄️ Winter crop
  • 💰 Price: ₹20-50/plant
  • 💵 Niche market

🌇 PUNE / MUMBAI FLOWER LIST (HEAT ADAPTED + MARKET DRIVEN)

  • 🔁 Life: 3-5 years
  • 🌸 Harvest: daily
  • 💰 Price: ₹3-10/stem
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹5-10 lakh/year

🌼 Gerbera - 6

  • ⏱️ Growth: ~3 months
  • 🔁 Life: 2-3 years
  • 🌸 Daily harvest
  • 💰 Price: ₹4-10/stem
  • 💵 Revenue: ₹5-9 lakh/year

  • ⚠️ Needs cooling

🌻 Sunflower (FAST CASH) - 7

  • 💵 Fast turnover
